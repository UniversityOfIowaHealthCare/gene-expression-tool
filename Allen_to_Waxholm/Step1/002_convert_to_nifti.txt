## Convert gene expression format to NIFTI Space
# Step1 folder contains: toolbox_nifti and mni.nii

1. Open Matlab
2. Add toolbox_matlab_nifti in the path
3. Insure, the desired gene expression map i.e. energy.raw in the current working directory. 
4. Insure, mni.nii is also in the current working directory. 
3. Run gene_in_nifti.m
